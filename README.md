# Supply Chain Performance & Demand Forecasting - DataCo

**Tools:** Python, SQL, Pandas, Scipy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Overview
End-to-end supply chain analysis of 180,000+ orders from the DataCo Smart Supply Chain dataset, covering sales performance, delivery risk analysis, market profitability, and demand forecasting.

## Key Findings
- First Class shipping has a 95.3% late delivery rate - the highest of any shipping mode
- Europe and LATAM lead sales at $10.8M and $10.3M respectively
- Discount rate shows no significant impact on delivery timeliness (p=0.67)
- Weak negative correlation between discount rate and profit (r=-0.019, p≈0)
- Field & Stream 16 Gun Fire Safe is the top revenue product at $6.9M

## Project Structure
- `supplychain_analysis.ipynb` - full analysis notebook
